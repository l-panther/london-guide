More London

London is the capital of England and is the most populous city of the United Kingdom. London is a leading global city in education, finance, fashion, media, health care, transportation and many other things. It is the world’s most visited city and has the world’s largest city airport system. London has a hugely diverse range of people and cultures, with more than 300 languages spoken around the city.

There are endless activities to see when you visit London from tourist attractions like the London Eye to bus tours. Everything is easily accessed and all activities will give more insight to diverse city. The multi cultural city has every to offer and you will find that there isn't enough hours in a day to do everything. It is recommended to plan and book early to ensure you don't miss out
